CATMON

Cebu, Philippines

AGOS, STEFFANY JANE

The Philippines is a country of islands and bright coastlines, rich traditions, and people known for hospitality. What makes it truly special is the warmth of Filipinos—friendly, welcoming, and family-centered. From everyday life to celebrations, there is a strong sense of unity that makes you feel at home.

Cebu is one of the places where this spirit shines. In Northern Cebu, my hometown Catmon is a quiet place with nature views and a relaxed vibe. Here, simple moments become meaningful—fresh air, familiar faces, and the comfort of local life.

Catmon may be small, but it has a unique charm. You can feel peace in the sea breeze, enjoy the green surroundings, and experience a slower pace that helps you appreciate the little things. The town is also known for local delicacies like bibingka and budbod kabog, which represent our tradition and the pride of our community.

Growing up in Catmon taught me to value simple happiness—sharing meals, helping neighbors, and spending time with family. Life here feels close and personal, where people recognize each other and treat visitors like friends.

This website shows my memories and my pride: my family, my friends, and my personal photos.

Thank you for visiting—welcome to my home, and welcome to Catmon, Cebu.
